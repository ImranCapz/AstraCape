Privacy Policy for Fishy

Effective Date: June 08, 2024

Thank you for choosing Fishy. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Fishy collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

User Information: When you use Fishy, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and location data.

Delivery Information: As a delivery personnel, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and location data to facilitate delivery services.

Admin Information: As an admin, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and phone number for administrative purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

User Information: We use your information to provide and personalize our services, process your orders, communicate with you, and improve our services.

Delivery Information: We use your information to facilitate delivery services, track deliveries, and communicate with you regarding delivery-related matters.

Admin Information: We use your information for administrative purposes, such as managing accounts, processing orders, and providing customer support.

3. Location Data

Fishy may collect and use your location data to provide location-based services, such as finding nearby fish stores, tracking deliveries, and optimizing routes for delivery personnel.

4. Google Login

Fishy uses Google login for authentication purposes. By using Google login, you authorize Fishy to access and use your Google account information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing

We may share your personal information with trusted third parties, such as delivery partners, payment processors, and service providers, to facilitate our services and improve your experience with Fishy.

6. Data Security

Fishy employs industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

7. Third-Party Libraries

Lottie: Used for animations to make the app more interactive and visually appealing.

ImageSlideshow: Used to create image sliders for displaying multiple images in a slideshow format.

Glide: Used for image loading and caching to provide a smooth and efficient experience when displaying images.

CircleImageView: Used to display images in a circular format, commonly used for profile pictures and other round image requirements.

CountryCodePicker: Used to provide an easy-to-use interface for selecting country codes, improving the user experience for entering phone numbers.

Navigation Component: Used to handle navigation within the app, providing a structured and consistent navigation experience.

8. Your Choices

You have the right to review, update, or delete your personal information stored by Fishy. You may also opt-out of certain communications or data sharing practices. Please contact us for assistance with your privacy preferences.

9. User Account Deletion Policy

Within the Fishy App, users have the possibility to delete their user accounts through its interface. To delete the user account, this option is available in the Settings section by accessing the Delete Account Menu. Fishyfy will not retain any data related to the user after their account has been deleted.

10. Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the revised policy on the Fishy app. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for the latest information on our privacy practices.
